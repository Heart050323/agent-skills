# WRITING checklist

Labels: 🔴 LOGIC GAP · 🟡 VERIFY · 🔵 SUGGESTION (severity scale in SKILL.md).

1. **Logical structure** — does the argument flow? Any gaps or contradictions?
2. **Factual accuracy** — a questionable claim triggers **Verify-before-Flag** (SKILL.md): search, don't trust memory.
3. **Clarity** — ambiguous sentences, or jargon left unexplained?
4. **Audience fit** — is the register right for the audience (academic / business / casual)?
5. **Completeness** — missing sections or unanswered questions the reader would expect?

End with a one-sentence overall assessment.
